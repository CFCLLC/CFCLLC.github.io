CFC Features

Host Family Program

Students will have a variety of cultural experiences through our well prepared host families. Living with local families will help students build their confidence and understand American culture.

Tutoring

Academic guidance and personalized tutoring to help students stay on top of their schoolwork, and their college application process (TOEFL, SAT, ACT, SAT Subject Test and AP exams preparation). .

Summer Program

California Foreign Connect partners with local schools to provide summer opportunities.

Tours

We include just the right amount of useful widget areas and sidebars so you can place your content.

Academic

California Foreign Connect also provides opportunities to visit different colleges and universities to help students figure out which school best fits their needs.

Cultural Immersion

Experience the benefits of learning about a new culture while living with American families, who open their homes and hearts to give you the opportunity to learn and practice the English language.

Student Support

We work with the schools and host families as a team to help students with schoolwork, extracurricular activities, and mentoring as they prepare for a successful college career.

Safety

Our students live with host families who have completed a safety training and background check.
